About Nordic Barista Cup

The vision within the Nordic Barisa cup is:

"To create an environment in which knowledge
about cofee and its sphere can be obtained"

"...create an environment..."
Combined with the personally absorption having
the opportunity to see and experince countries,
people, traditions etc. will allways serve as a
source of inspiration in our daily work.
The orginization behind the Nordic Barista Cup
see it as its main purpose to be a part of creating
this forum in which people can meet, bond and
achieve further knowledge.
